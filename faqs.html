Frequently Asked Questions

My institution is not on the list.

If your institution is not on our list, please contact our support team or you can add it yourself (please check the demo page for more information.).

As an instructor, how can I create a case study?

Please check the demo page for more information.

Subscribe as an institution

If you're an institution interested in subscribing, we can set up access for your entire organization. This allows all your instructors and students to use our platform free of charge. Please contact our support team .

Case study is not included with citations.

Our next update will include citations for all case studies.

Case study variety is limited

In the following months, we will add more case study creation options to our platform. You will specifically be able to create case studies with the following options:

  • Conversation-Based
  • Facing a Dilemma and Making a Decision
  • Evaluating Multiple Options
  • Role-Playing Scenarios
  • Investigating a Situation
  • Encountering a Crisis
  • Complete the Story
  • Running a Project
  • Launching a New Product or Service

The promotion code is not working.

Please make sure you have entered the correct code. If you are still having trouble, please contact our support team .

How much does one case study cost for students?

One average, one case study costs between $1.99 and $4.99 for students. It depends on the complexity of the case study and the number of insights provided. It also depend if you are using our evaluation and feedback system. Please contact our support team , we can offer an incredible discount of up to 50% for the first semester.

How I can create case study without adding a course

For now, you can create a case study only by adding a course. In the following months, you will be able to create a case study without adding a course. You can also create advanced case studies without a watermark and by securly uploading your own data (i.e., interviews).

How do I cancel my subscription?

Please contact our support team to cancel your subscription.
